deployment.apps/nginx-deploy created

NAME           READY   UP-TO-DATE   AVAILABLE   AGE
nginx-deploy   2/2     2            2           15s

varun@LAPTOP:~$ kubectl rollout status deployment nginx-deploy
deployment "nginx-deploy" successfully rolled out


Advantages:

Maintains a stable number of replicas.

Automatically replaces failed pods.




Disadvantages:

Does not support label selectors with set-based requirements.

Lacks support for rolling updates.

Mostly replaced by ReplicaSet/Deployment.
